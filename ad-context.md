# BytLinks — Ad Generation Context File

Use this document as the source of truth when generating ad creative, copy, scripts, or campaigns for BytLinks.

## 1. PRODUCT SUMMARY

**BytLinks** is a link-in-bio platform that turns a single URL into a fully customizable personal page. Users add links, social profiles, and content blocks — and choose from 12 genuinely distinct design styles. Built-in analytics track views, clicks, referrers, and devices without third-party scripts.

**URL:** bytlinks.com
**Category:** Link-in-bio / personal micro-website
**Primary tagline:** "Your link. Your brand. Your data."
**Hero headline:** "One link. Everything people need."

## 2. WHAT MAKES BYTLINKS DIFFERENT

These are the points of real differentiation — not marketing fluff. Use these as the backbone for ad messaging.

### A. 12 Truly Different Design Styles (All Free)
Not color swaps. Each style has its own typography, layout philosophy, spacing, and personality:
- **Minimal** — Apple-esque whitespace, thin sans-serif
- **Bold Type** — Oversized editorial typography
- **Dark Pro** — Deep dark with sharp accents (dev/tech)
- **Glass** — Glassmorphism panels, modern & airy
- **Brutalist** — Raw borders, monospace, no softness
- **Editorial** — Magazine column feel, serif body
- **Soft Warm** — Rounded corners, warm pastels
- **Neon Night** — Dark + vivid neon (music/gaming)
- **Paper** — Off-white, ink-like, analog texture
- **Gradient Flow** — Full-bleed smooth gradients
- **Grid** — Visible grid structure, geometric
- **Retro** — Muted vintage palette, display slab

Competitors paywall their themes. BytLinks gives every style free.

### B. Desktop Is a First-Class Experience
Most link-in-bio tools are mobile-only stretched to desktop. BytLinks renders a real two-column grid on desktop with a sticky sidebar — it feels like a personal website, not a phone screen on a monitor.

### C. Privacy-First Analytics, Built In
Server-side tracking — no cookies, no third-party scripts. Every user gets:
- Page views + per-link click counts (free)
- Referrers, countries, devices, click-through rates, real-time pulse (Pro)

### D. 18 Content Block Types
Go beyond links. Embed YouTube videos, add FAQ accordions, run polls, show testimonials, accept newsletter signups, sell downloads, display countdown timers, and more.

### E. Deep Customization Without Complexity
- 11 button styles (filled, outline, pill, brutalist, gradient, etc.)
- 8 font pairs (curated, no generic defaults)
- 8 entrance animations + 5 speed settings
- 5 animated backgrounds (night sky, rain, fireflies, bokeh, waves)
- Per-link style overrides (shape, color, text color)
- Custom color palettes (15 presets + full custom)

## 6. VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Brand Voice
- **Confident, not arrogant.** We know the product is good. We don't need to shout.
- **Clean, not clinical.** Professional without being corporate. Warm without being twee.
- **Technical-adjacent, not nerdy.** Our audience appreciates craft. Don't dumb it down, but don't gatekeep either.
- **Direct.** Say it in fewer words. Lead with the value, not the setup.

### Tone by Channel
| Channel | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Instagram/TikTok ads** | Punchy, visual-first, aspirational | "Stop sending people to a boring link page." |
| **X / Twitter ads** | Sharp, confident, slightly opinionated | "Your Linktree looks like everyone else's. That's the problem." |
| **YouTube pre-roll** | Quick hook, show don't tell, demo-driven | "Watch me build a page that doesn't look AI-generated. 30 seconds." |
| **Google Search** | Benefit-focused, clear CTA | "Free link-in-bio with 12 design styles. No credit card." |
| **Email / Newsletter** | Conversational, value-forward | "We built the link page we wanted but couldn't find." |

### Words We Use
- "your page" (not "your profile" or "your Linktree")
- "design styles" (not "templates" — they're more than that)
- "content blocks" (not "widgets")
- "claim your page" (not "sign up")
- "built-in analytics" (not "tracking")

### Words We Avoid
- "revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "next-gen"
- "powered by AI" (we're not an AI product)
- "unlimited customization" (be specific about what's customizable)
- "link-in-bio tool" as the primary descriptor (we're "a personal page platform")

## 9. VISUAL DIRECTION FOR ADS

### What to Show
- **Side-by-side comparisons:** Generic link page vs. BytLinks page (same links, wildly different look)
- **Style diversity reel:** Rapid cuts between 12 styles applied to the same page content
- **Desktop + mobile split:** Show the real two-column desktop layout alongside mobile
- **Speed demo:** Screen recording of building a page from signup to published in <60 seconds
- **Customization depth:** Cycling through button styles, font pairs, background effects
- **Analytics dashboard:** Clean charts showing referrers, countries, real-time activity

### Visual Don'ts
- No purple/violet/indigo gradients (these scream "AI-generated")
- No generic stock photos of people pointing at screens
- No floating UI elements with heavy drop shadows on gradient backgrounds
- No three-column icon grids (Linktree-style feature dumps)
- Avoid Inter, Roboto, or system fonts in ad creative — use the brand fonts (Cabinet Grotesk / DM Sans) or the theme fonts

### Brand Colors for Ad Creative
- **Primary accent:** Teal/Emerald (#0d9488)
- **Background:** Warm stone (#fafaf9)
- **Text:** Deep slate (#1c1917)
- **Secondary surface:** Light gray (#f5f5f4)

## 14. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC AD FORMATS

### Instagram / TikTok (Short-form video)
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical, 15-30 seconds
- **Hook (first 3 seconds):** Show a generic Linktree → "This is what your audience sees." Cut to BytLinks page → "This is what they could see."
- **Body:** Quick style cycling (3-4 styles in 5 seconds), show one block type, flash the analytics dashboard
- **CTA:** "Link in bio" or "Claim your page — free"

### YouTube Pre-Roll (6-15 second)
- **6s bumper:** "Your link page can look like this." [Show stunning BytLinks page] "BytLinks. Free."
- **15s:** "Every link-in-bio page looks the same. [pause] Yours doesn't have to." [Show 3 styles rapidly] "12 design styles. Built-in analytics. Free forever. BytLinks."

### X / Twitter
- **Image ad:** Split-screen — generic link page vs. BytLinks page. Caption: "Same links. Different impression."
- **Text ad:** "We built 12 completely different link page styles and made them all free. Because paywalling aesthetics is weird. → bytlinks.com"

### Google Search
- **Headline 1:** "Free Link-in-Bio Page | 12 Design Styles"
- **Headline 2:** "BytLinks — Your Link, Your Brand"
- **Description:** "Create a beautiful personal page in 60 seconds. 12 unique styles, built-in analytics, 18 content blocks. Free forever, no credit card required."

### Facebook / Meta
- **Carousel:** Each card = one design style applied to the same page content. Final card = CTA.
- **Single image:** Desktop + mobile side-by-side showing real layout. Caption focuses on "not just mobile."
